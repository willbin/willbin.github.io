Rage Daily Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 14, 2026

Summary

Rage Daily (内涵暴走漫画) is an offline-first comic browser and on-device comic maker operated by Wecomic. No account is required. The app does not provide a public feed, comments, followers, chat, or automatic upload of creations.

Information stored on your device

The app stores favorites, recent comic identifiers, hidden comic identifiers, daily unlock progress, preferences, and Maker drafts on your device. Photos you choose through Apple's photo picker are used in the Maker project you create. Rage Daily does not upload those photos, captions, drafts, or exported comics to our own server.

Sharing and reports

A comic, Maker export, or report leaves the app only after you choose an action through an Apple share sheet, Mail, Files, Photos, or another destination. The destination you choose has its own privacy practices. Content reports include the relevant comic identifier and any details you enter.

Advertising and consent

The free app may display ads supplied by Google Mobile Ads. Where required, Google User Messaging Platform presents a regional consent or privacy message before ads are requested. Publisher ad personalization and publisher first-party ID are disabled in the app's current configuration.

Google and its advertising partners may process information such as IP-derived coarse location, device identifiers, ad impressions and interactions, product interaction, crash data, performance data, and other diagnostics to deliver, limit, secure, and measure advertising. The exact processing depends on region, consent choice, device settings, and Google's services.

Learn more in Google's Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services.

Your choices

  • Use Settings in the app to clear favorites, recent history, and hidden choices.
  • Delete Maker drafts from My Drafts.
  • Delete exported files from the destination where you saved them.
  • Where required, reopen Manage Privacy Choices from Settings > Content & safety.
  • You may use the core browser and Maker even when ads are unavailable or a privacy choice prevents an ad request.

Children

Rage Daily is not directed to children and is not offered in Apple's Kids Category. The final App Store age rating reflects the reviewed content included in the app.

Retention

Local app data remains on your device until you remove it, reset it in the app, or uninstall the app. Google controls retention for information processed by its advertising and consent services under its policies.

Changes

We may update this policy when the app, SDKs, or legal requirements change. The effective date above identifies the current version.

内涵暴走漫画隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 7 月 14 日

概要

内涵暴走漫画是一款离线优先的漫画浏览器和设备本地漫画制作器，由 Wecomic 运营。App 无需注册账号，也没有公开动态、评论、关注、聊天或自动上传作品的功能。

保存在设备上的信息

App 会在设备上保存收藏、最近浏览的漫画标识、隐藏漫画标识、每日解锁进度、偏好设置和制作器草稿。你通过 Apple 照片选择器选择的照片只用于你创建的制作项目。内涵暴走漫画不会把这些照片、字幕、草稿或导出漫画上传到我们自己的服务器。

分享与举报

只有在你主动选择 Apple 系统分享面板、邮件、文件、照片或其他目标后，漫画、制作器导出内容或举报信息才会离开 App。你选择的目标服务有自己的隐私规则。内容举报会包含相关漫画标识及你主动填写的说明。

广告与同意

免费版可能展示由 Google 移动广告提供的广告。在适用地区，Google User Messaging Platform 会在请求广告前显示相应的同意或隐私消息。当前 App 配置已关闭发布商广告个性化和发布商第一方 ID。

Google 及其广告合作伙伴可能会处理基于 IP 推断的大致位置、设备标识、广告展示与互动、产品互动、崩溃数据、性能数据及其他诊断信息，用于投放、限制、保护和衡量广告。实际处理方式取决于所在地区、你的同意选择、设备设置以及 Google 服务。

详情请参阅 Google 隐私权政策以及使用 Google 服务的网站或应用如何使用信息。

你的选择

  • 在 App 设置中清除收藏、最近浏览和隐藏选择。
  • 在“我的草稿”中删除制作器草稿。
  • 在你保存导出内容的目标位置删除相应文件。
  • 在有相关要求的地区，可从“设置 > 内容与安全”重新打开“管理隐私选项”。
  • 即使广告不可用或隐私选择不允许请求广告，核心浏览与制作功能仍可使用。

儿童

内涵暴走漫画并非面向儿童，也不会加入 Apple 儿童类别。最终 App Store 年龄分级会根据实际审核后的内置内容填写。

保留期限

设备本地数据会一直保留，直到你主动删除、在 App 内重置或卸载 App。Google 会依据其政策决定广告与同意服务所处理信息的保留期限。

政策更新

当 App、第三方 SDK 或法律要求发生变化时，我们可能更新本政策。页面顶部的生效日期表示当前版本。
