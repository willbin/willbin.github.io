Before contacting support

  • Include the comic number shown in the detail screen for content or copyright reports.
  • For an ad report, use Settings > Advertising > Report an Ad so the response identifier is included.
  • For a Maker issue, include the layout, number of panels, iOS version and whether a bundled image or personal photo was used.
  • Do not email private photos unless they are essential to the report.

Common questions

Does Rage Daily download new comics every day?

No. The catalog is bundled with the app and is revealed gradually using an on-device local schedule.

Where are my drafts?

Open Maker, choose the folder menu, then My Drafts. Drafts remain on this device.

How do I restore a hidden comic?

Open My Stuff, select Hidden, then tap Restore.

How do I delete local data?

Use Settings > Reset local data for favorites, recent history and hidden choices. Delete Maker drafts separately in My Drafts.

How do I change language?

Rage Daily follows the supported language selected for the app or device in iOS Settings.

常见问题

每天都会下载新漫画吗？

不会。漫画库已经内置在 App 中，只是按照设备本地计划逐步开放。

草稿在哪里？

打开“制作”，点击文件夹菜单，再选择“我的草稿”。草稿只保存在当前设备上。

如何恢复隐藏的漫画？

打开“我的”，选择“隐藏”，再点击“恢复”。

如何删除本地数据？

在设置中重置收藏、最近浏览和隐藏选择；制作器草稿需要在“我的草稿”中单独删除。

如何更改语言？

内涵暴走漫画会跟随 iOS 设置中为 App 或设备选择的受支持语言。
